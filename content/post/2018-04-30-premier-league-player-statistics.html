Treble accuracy

Figure 1: 95% confidence intervals for the probability of each player to hit the treble they were aiming for. A player can be considered to have a significantly different probability than another player if their respective confidence intervals do not overlap. For example, Michael van Gerwen’s interval ranges from 0.45 to 0.50, whereas Daryl Gurney’s interval ranges from 0.38 to 0.43, therefore MVG has a significantly higher probability of hitting a treble than Daryl Gurney.
